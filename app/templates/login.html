About FriendInNeed.

FriendsInNeed creates a composite sentiment score of how you have been feeling over the course of the last 30 days. Using the natural language processing services of IBM’s Bluemix Alchemy API, user’s posts on both Twitter and Facebook are evaluated for both sentiment and emotions. The composite rating is calculated using an average of ratings from Twitter and Facebook weighted based on frequency of posts.
